Why TBD?

No Cost


Every process in this program, from finding your perfect passion project, to applying an interest form, and finally submitting your fabulous work is completely free of cost. Students don't need to spend money to do what they like.

Find Interest


TBD offers over 20 different real life problems for students to come up with a solution for, each problem from a different industry or field. Can't find one that is interesting? Pose your own real life problem and propose a solution.

Freedom


You decide how you want to propose your solution. It can be in the form of an essay, a video, a computer program, or in another unique way. Sky is the limit for your creativity and approach. You are given the freedom to express yourself.

Benefits


Get recognition for you awesome work. Get rewarded for your time spent in forms of scholarships. Receive community service hours. Create projects that can be put on your portfolio, or resume. And so much more.

Portfolio


You can add the work you've done to your resume or portfolio to showcase your skills, effort, commitment, passion. Work a project that is meaningful to you and passionate about, to add depth to your college applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

TBD is a non-profit organization that provides highschoolers from around the world with specific projects from different industries and fields. The projects often involve students proposing a solution to a real-life problem, or improvise on existing solutions.

Every process is completely free, from filling out an application form to submitting the final project.

There are many. As a non-profit organization, TBD can give students volunteering hours for your determination and effort. Money, in the form of scholarships will also be offered to students who display an exceptional work through their final submission of their work. Recognition will be given for all the students who participated. Last but certainly not the least, the experience and knowledge they get from successfully finishing their project will be significant.

Currently, TBD is accepting all highschoolers who are passionate and interested in pursuing a project.

All the projects are there for high schoolers to either help them gain more knowledge, provide a valuable experience, or help them find their passion. Ultimately, the key factors are effort, dedication, and consistency. If all those factors are above standards, including these projects as part of activities and extracurriculars list will certainly help admission officers understand students better.

Currently, TBD is accepting all highschoolers from the United States of America. We certainly hope to expand our platform to more countries in the near future. Stay tuned :)

All high school students who are currently living in the United States (citizen, permanent resident, legal resident).
